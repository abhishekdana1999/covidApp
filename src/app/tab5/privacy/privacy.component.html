Privacy A Privacy Policy can be used for both your website and mobile app if it's adapted to include the platforms your business operates on. The requirements for Privacy Policies may differ from one country to another depending on the legislation. However, most privacy laws identify the following critical points that a business must comply with when dealing with personal data: Notice - Data collectors must clearly disclose what they are doing with the personal information from users before collecting it. Choice - The companies collecting the data must respect the choices of users on what information they choose to provide. Access - Users should be able to view, update or request the removal of personal data collected by the company. Security - Companies are entirely responsible for the accuracy and security (keeping it properly away from unauthorized eyes and hands) of the collected personal information.
